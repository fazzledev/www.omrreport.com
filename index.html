Why OMR Report

Three things we do that others don't.

01

100% accuracy on
real-world prints

Smudged bubbles. Low-quality photocopies. Slightly rotated scans. Missing corners. Sheets fed through broken scanners and rickety xerox machines. Other OMR software chokes on these. We don't.

Our processing engine handles the reality of a coaching centre print room — not the ideal conditions of a demo.

02

Results in minutes,
not days

Upload scanned OMR sheets. Get complete results, rank lists, and analytics within minutes. Not hours. Not the next day.

Your students get feedback while the test is still fresh. Your teachers can discuss papers the same day. The academic cycle tightens.

03

Question Paper Discussions
Like Never Before

After every test, your teachers sit with students for question paper discussion. They need to know: which questions did most students get wrong? Which topics are weak across the batch? Which were the easy marks that students still missed?

Our analytics are designed around this exact workflow. Not as a feature. As the foundation.
